# Import Tetreum (TET)

### Contract Address

`0x8C8407a0Eca05B481bFb13C0E5061b3Be775Be55`

[https://bscscan.com/token/0x8C8407a0Eca05B481bFb13C0E5061b3Be775Be55](https://bscscan.com/token/0x8C8407a0Eca05B481bFb13C0E5061b3Be775Be55)

***

### MetaMask

1. Open MetaMask
2. Switch to **BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)**
3. Click **Import Tokens**
4. Paste contract address
5. Click **Add Custom Token → Import**

***

### Trust Wallet

1. Open Trust Wallet
2. Tap top-right icon
3. Tap **Add Custom Token**
4. Select **Smart Chain**
5. Paste contract address
6. Fill if needed:
   * Name: Tetreum
   * Symbol: TET
   * Decimals: 18
7. Tap **Save**

***

### Notes

* Works only on **BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)**
* Wrong network = token will not show
* Always verify contract address

***

### Done

Your TET balance will appear in your wallet.
