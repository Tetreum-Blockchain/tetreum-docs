# How to Buy Tetreum (TET)

### 1. Create a Wallet

To buy TET, you need a **self-custody wallet**.

Supported wallets include:

* MetaMask
* Trust Wallet

#### Steps

1. Download **MetaMask** or **Trust Wallet** from the official website or app store.
2. Create a new wallet.
3. Back up your **seed phrase** securely.
4. Never share your private key or recovery phrase with anyone.

> \[!NOTE] Do not use exchange wallets directly. Always use a wallet that you control.

***

### 2. Get BNB

BNB is commonly used to participate in the Tetreum presale and pay network gas fees.

#### Buy BNB

You can buy BNB from trusted exchanges such as:

* Binance
* Crypto.com
* Kraken
* MoonPay

After buying, transfer your BNB to your personal wallet.

> \[!IMPORTANT] Use **BEP-20 (BNB Smart Chain)** when sending BNB to your wallet.

***

### 3. Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit the official Tetreum website:\
   **https://tetreum.com**
2. Click **Connect Wallet**
3. Select your wallet
4. Approve the wallet connection

Make sure your wallet is connected to the correct network before proceeding.

***

### 4. Buy TET

1. Enter the amount you want to spend
2. Select your payment asset
3. Review how many **TET** you will receive
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet

> \[!TIP] Keep a small amount of BNB in your wallet for gas fees.

***

### 5. Supported Payment Options

Tetreum currently supports purchases with:

* USDT
* ETH
* BNB
* SOL
* USDC

Availability may vary depending on wallet and network setup.

***

### 6. Stay Safe

* Only use the official website: **https://tetreum.com**
* Never share your seed phrase
* Double-check wallet prompts before confirming
* Avoid unofficial links, fake support accounts, and copied domains

***

### 7. You're In

Once your purchase is complete, you are part of the Tetreum ecosystem.

You can then follow future announcements regarding:

* token access
* ecosystem participation
* staking
* governance
* upcoming utilities
